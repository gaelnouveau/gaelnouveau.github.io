Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Video Creator Pro ("we", "us", or "our") operates an automated content creation and publishing tool that integrates with TikTok via the official TikTok API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Video Creator Pro, we may collect:

  • TikTok account identifiers (user ID, username)
  • OAuth access tokens required for API authentication
  • Video publishing metadata (publish ID, timestamps, status)
  • Basic usage data for performance tracking purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok's Content Posting API on your behalf
  • Publish AI-generated videos to your TikTok account
  • Track publishing performance and maintain activity logs
  • Send email notifications about published content

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal information to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

Video Creator Pro uses the TikTok Content Posting API. We access only the permissions explicitly granted by you during the OAuth authorization flow. We access the following TikTok scopes:

  • video.publish — to publish videos to your TikTok account
  • video.upload — to upload video content
  • user.info.basic — to retrieve basic profile information

We do not access your TikTok messages, contacts, or any data beyond what is necessary for content publishing.

4. Data Storage & Security

Access tokens and account identifiers are stored securely and are never shared with unauthorized parties. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Publishing logs are stored in private Google Sheets accessible only to authorized account holders.

5. Data Retention

We retain access tokens only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions." Upon revocation, we will no longer be able to publish content on your behalf.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your TikTok account at any time
  • Request deletion of any stored data related to your account
  • Contact us with questions or concerns about your data

7. Third-Party Services

Video Creator Pro uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • TikTok API (content publishing)
  • OpenAI API (script generation and voiceover)
  • D-ID API (AI avatar video generation)
  • Apify (trend data collection)
  • Google Sheets (performance logging)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@videocreatorpro.app
